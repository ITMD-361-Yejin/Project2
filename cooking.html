Potato Salad

This salad is the easiest one to make. You can pick pretty potatoes and boil them. While cooking them, you can chop spring onions. Peel the boiled potatoes and put the spring onions on them. I know you already have black peppers, salt, and olive oil at your home, so you can add on it as much as you want!
